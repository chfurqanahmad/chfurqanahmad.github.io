Public-Source Research & Verification

A structured public-source assessment designed to demonstrate careful research, source comparison and transparent limitations.

This page is an honest project outline, not a claim that the case study is already complete. It will be updated as public-safe evidence is produced.

Objective

Select a public organization or topic and answer a clearly defined research question using reliable, accessible sources.

Planned methodology

  • Define scope, research question and decision criteria.
  • Create a source log with dates, URLs and notes.
  • Separate confirmed facts, reasonable inferences and unresolved questions.
  • Compare important claims across more than one source.
  • Provide findings, limitations and a practical recommendation.

Planned deliverables

  • Research brief
  • Source-validation table
  • Key findings and risk notes
  • One-page decision summary

Confidentiality approach

The final project will use only public information and will not reproduce private investigation files, personal client data or confidential employer material.
