Prospect Qualification & Personalized Outreach

A fictional qualification and messaging exercise showing how relevance, authority and personalization can guide responsible outreach.

This page is an honest project outline, not a claim that the case study is already complete. It will be updated as public-safe evidence is produced.

Objective

Build a fictional prospect list for a sample health-technology organization and prioritize contacts using transparent qualification criteria.

Planned methodology

  • Define sector, stage, geography and role requirements.
  • Verify current role and likely decision authority.
  • Score strategic relevance using a simple qualification framework.
  • Identify a genuine personalization angle from public information.
  • Draft distinct outreach messages without mass-automation language.

Planned deliverables

  • Fictional prospect matrix
  • Qualification scorecard
  • Personalization notes
  • Connection-note and follow-up examples

Ethical boundary

No real private investor lists, confidential AlphaRose data or unpublished conversations will be included. The project will be clearly labeled as fictional or sanitized.
