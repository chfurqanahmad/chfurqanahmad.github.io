About

Connecting research, quality and practical testing.

My background combines investigative research, report quality review, analyst training, prospect outreach and hands-on mobile device testing, supported by an earlier foundation in software and database development.

I work best where information needs to be found, checked, organized and translated into a useful next step. My experience includes public-source research, investigative reporting, quality auditing, process improvement, prospect qualification and personalized outreach.

I also test mobile products in real-world conditions, documenting software bugs, usability concerns and performance observations. Across these areas, my approach remains consistent: understand the objective, verify the evidence, communicate clearly and protect confidential information.

Structured research Start with a clear question, track sources and separate evidence from assumptions.
Quality-minded review Check accuracy, completeness, consistency and usefulness before delivery.
Human outreach Prioritize relevance and personalization rather than volume or generic automation.
